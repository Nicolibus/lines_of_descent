Mutation probability ⓘ

Determines the occurrence of new individuals with different genetic makeup than the initial population.
Bottleneck probability ⓘ

Indicates the frequency of events (e.g. famine, earthquakes, disease) that would cause a sharp decrease in the population size.
Reproduction probability ⓘ

Controls the growth of the population.
About

This is a visualization of the genetic bottleneck effect on an imaginary population. Every row is a population generation which can be added with a click.
